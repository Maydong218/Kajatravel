Destinations Most visited

Geoje Island

You'll start by going to Windy Hill on Geoje Island, where you may take in the breathtaking views from the top of the hill, which includes a typical windmill. Snap advantage of some free time to explore the island, take photographs, and smell the many flowers and flora. Climb the Stairway to Heaven and take in the breathtaking views of the sea.

Tongyeong

Fresh fish and traditional Korean cuisine are popular in Tongyeong. Two of Tongyeong's most interesting activities are waiting for you after the market and village visit. You'll toboggan down Skyline Luge after riding Korea's longest cable car. Views of the sea may be seen from the cable car, and Skyline Luge offers a one-of-a-kind adventure in Korea.

Sightseeing Cruise

While sailing past Busan, keep an eye out for the bright lighthouses dotting the coastline, as well as Saengdo Island, which is shaped like a kettle. Before the boat swings around and travels back to the original launching point, take in the amazing views of Taejongdae Park's beautiful cliffs.
