Destinations Most visited

Ganghwa Peace Observatory

The Ganghwa Peace Observatory offers the most up-close and clear view of North Korean farmlands. Walk through the mostly undeveloped trails, which are surrounded by barbed wire, and stop by 6.25 Veterans Memorial Park to hear about the terrible history of the Korean War.

Imjingak Park

Imjingak Park, a section of the Paju DMZ, contains various sculptures and monuments commemorating the Korean War. Discover the Bridge of Freedom, which was utilized by POWs and troops who were returned.

Veterans Memorial Park

Walk through the mostly undeveloped trails, which are surrounded by barbed wire, and stop by 6.25 Veterans Memorial Park to hear about the terrible history of the Korean War. Close your eyes for a moment and reflect on their sacrifices.
