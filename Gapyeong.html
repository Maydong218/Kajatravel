Destinations Most visited

Nami Island

Both residents and visitors go to Nami Island for a relaxing vacation. The region is well-known for its beautiful tree-lined streets. On the island, you may participate in a variety of activities or simply rest and take in the breathtaking scenery.

Petite Francek

Petite France is a colorful French town with brilliantly colored houses that make it stand out. This town is home to several photo-worthy locations that have appeared in dramas and variety shows such as "Beethoven Virus," "Secret Garden," "Running Man," and, most recently, "You Who Came From The Stars."
