A 10-hour journey outside of Seoul will take you to the must-see sights. Namiseom, Petite France, and The Garden of the Morning Calm are three of the most popular sights to visit. Rent a rail bike to peddle along historic railway trails.

Out for Namiseom, a picturesque and well-known South Korean location. Explore the half-moon-shaped island, which features beautiful views and was the setting for the popular Korean drama Winter Sonata. Walk around the island at your own leisure to get a feel for it.

On to The Garden of Morning Calm, a beautiful garden that hosts a number of festivals throughout the year, attracting families, friends, couples, and photographers. At the nearby restaurants, try some local fare.
