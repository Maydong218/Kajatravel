Best occasion

South Korea has four distinct seasons: a dry, chilly winter from November to March, a lush spring from March to late May, a humid and rainy monsoon season from June to August, and a crisp autumn from September to November. The greatest times to visit South Korea are in the spring and autumn, but winter sports fans should visit during the winter months, when the country's numerous resorts and beautiful powder make it an ideal winter escape. Summer is the most difficult time to visit South Korea due to the heat and people.
